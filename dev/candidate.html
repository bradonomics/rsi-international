About RSI International Asia

  • RSI International Asia is an executive search firm which specialises in senior management positions in established and emerging SE Asian markets.
  • With proven and successful methodologies, we are able to provide highly customised executive search services across key disciplines.
  • We pride ourselves on our high quality, consultative yet results-oriented approach to the search process which allows our clients to make key strategic hires in the most informed and efficient manner.
  • We have a team of experienced and deeply networked consultants with proven track records of success within their specialist disciplines.
  • We continually endeavour to surpass client and candidate expectations

Our Strengths

Multicultural expertise. Local knowledge, Global outlook, Asian perspective. RSI International Asia is a multicultural business with a multinational client base.

Our clients benefit from our multicultural approach, because our consultants are deeply embedded in their local market. Their local knowledge is enhanced by real world experience in the commercial arena. They understand and know the reality of the environment in terms of business issues, implementation challenges and finding excellent candidates. More importantly we understand the market conditions and are able to give concise and accurate advice on attracting the best talent as well as the latest remuneration trends. Our scope includes a broad range of expertise across different industries, encompassing local and SE Asian markets. Having extensive local experience and understanding of how business operates in Asia has enabled us to harness the true wealth of our local knowledge and capabilities. Together, we offer an in depth perspective that recognises the challenges and opportunities of a diverse executive population; complex regional and local competition and the commercial priorities that drive client needs.

Our Expertise

Our consultants have extreme in-depth knowledge of the sectors in which they operate. In the ever changing business environment, it is essential to have a clear and up to date understanding of markets, challenges posed and the market dynamics. When it comes to selecting outstanding talent that can quickly add value to your organisation, we ensure that we really understand the markets in which our clients operate.

RSI International Asia has a number of areas of specialisation. Within these specialist areas, our consultants have in depth industry knowledge and functional expertise which allows them to better understand client requirements and the need to deliver service excellence coupled with highly skilled and qualified candidates.

What we do

Whether you’re searching for your dream job or looking to recruit the ideal person for your business, we can help. At RSI International Asia we bring job seekers and employers together.

Our Mission

To partner with the senior management of premium companies with established or newly started operations in SE Asia to help them execute key appointments aimed at elevating their business to the next level.

To be the trusted advisor to our clients on topics of leadership, search & assessment, market change, corporate culture, and talent management.

To consistently deliver executive recruitment and leadership solutions to our clients and to do so faster and better than our competitors. To undertake a professional, ethical and trustworthy approach with complete transparency for both clients and candidates.

Essence of Our Work

Professional Ethics; RSI International Asia is committed to integrity and the highest levels of professionalism. We place relationships and business ethics at the forefront of everything that we do.

Striving For Excellence; we strive to deliver the highest quality performance in everything we do.

Optimal long-term results; we seek to align our clients’ missions, cultures, strategic goals and key requirements with the right candidate’s professional track record, behavioral profile and ambition.

Excellence in Process; we liaise very closely with our clients and candidates at each stage of the recruitment process to ensure swift and accurate identification as well as effective selection, closing and assimilation.

Teamwork; our success at RSI International Asia is underpinned by the culture of ASEAN liberalisation, teamwork and partnerships which are the fundamentals of our Business.
